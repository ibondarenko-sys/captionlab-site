SRT vs burned-in captions for social

TikTok, Reels, and Shorts generally expect burned-in (visible-by-default) captions rather than a toggleable SRT track. Generate the SRT with CaptionLab, then use it as the basis for burned-in text — either through Premiere Pro's caption-to-graphic conversion, or by styling it in whatever caption-animation workflow you already use for social cuts.
