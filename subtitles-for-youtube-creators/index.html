Frequently asked questions

Do subtitles affect YouTube SEO?
Caption text is searchable text associated with your video, which can help with discoverability, though it's one of many ranking factors, not a guaranteed boost.
Should I upload an SRT or burn captions into the video?
SRT upload lets viewers toggle captions on/off and supports YouTube's auto-translation; burned-in captions are always visible, which works well for short-form/social-style content.
Can I get subtitles in a language YouTube doesn't auto-generate?
Yes — CaptionLab covers 99 languages via Whisper, well beyond what YouTube's own auto-captions reliably support.
