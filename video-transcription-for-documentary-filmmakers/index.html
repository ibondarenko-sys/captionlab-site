Frequently asked questions

Can it transcribe hours of raw interview footage at once?
Yes — select a full sequence or a long clip and CaptionLab processes it in one pass, respecting any gaps or cuts already in your timeline.
Does it handle multiple interview subjects in one file?
It transcribes everything said, but doesn't currently label who's speaking (no speaker diarization) — useful for content, less so for automatically separating speakers.
Is this suitable for protecting confidential sources?
Transcription happens entirely on your own machine with no upload step, which removes one common risk — but overall source protection depends on your full security practices, not just this tool.
