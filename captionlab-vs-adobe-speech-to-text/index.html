Frequently asked questions

Do I need to cancel Creative Cloud to use CaptionLab?
No — CaptionLab is a Premiere Pro extension, it runs alongside your existing Creative Cloud apps. You just don't need Adobe's Speech to Text for captioning specifically.
Is CaptionLab as accurate as Adobe's Speech to Text?
Both are built on modern speech-recognition models. CaptionLab uses OpenAI's Whisper, which is widely regarded as one of the most accurate open speech-recognition models available, including for accented and non-English speech.
Can I use both Adobe's tool and CaptionLab?
Yes, nothing stops you from using either per project — many editors use Adobe's tool for English content and CaptionLab for everything else.
