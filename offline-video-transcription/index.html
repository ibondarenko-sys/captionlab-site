Frequently asked questions

Is offline transcription slower than cloud transcription?
It depends on your hardware, but with an Apple Silicon Mac or an NVIDIA GPU on Windows, local transcription is often comparable to or faster than uploading a large file and waiting in a cloud queue.
Does offline mean lower accuracy?
No — CaptionLab runs the same Whisper large-v2 model used by many cloud transcription services, just locally instead of on someone else's server.
