Questions

Before you ask

Do I need an internet connection?
Only once — to download the Whisper model during setup (~1.5 GB). After that, CaptionLab runs 100% offline. No internet, no servers, no nothing.
Do I need an API key or an OpenAI account?
No. Unlike cloud tools, CaptionLab runs the Whisper model locally on your Mac. No Deepgram, no Groq, no OpenAI credits. Nothing to sign up for.
Is my footage uploaded anywhere?
Never. Every byte of audio is processed on your own machine. Perfect for client work under NDA.
Which Premiere Pro versions are supported?
Premiere Pro 2023 through 2026 (versions 23.x–26.x). Works on Apple Silicon (M1–M4) and Intel Macs.
Is there a Windows version?
In active development. Right now CaptionLab is Mac-only. Join the list and you'll be first to know when Windows drops.
What languages are supported?
All 99 languages Whisper supports — including Ukrainian, Polish, Turkish, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Arabic, Thai, Romanian, Czech, Slovak, Hungarian, Greek, Hebrew, Persian, Urdu, and dozens more.
