Questions

Before you ask

Does it really work offline?
Yes. One-time setup downloads the AI engine (~3 GB). After that — fully offline. Your audio and footage never leave your Mac. Plane, remote shoot, no wi-fi: it works.
Which languages exactly?
All 99 Whisper languages: Ukrainian, Polish, Turkish, Arabic, Hebrew, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Hindi, Greek, Czech, Romanian, Serbian, Kazakh, Persian… plus all the "big" ones. If you can say it, it can subtitle it.
How accurate is it?
CaptionLab runs OpenAI's Whisper large model — state of the art for speech recognition, on par with or better than most cloud services. Clean audio gives near-perfect results; you can always fine-tune the caption track in Premiere.
Is it really not a subscription?
Really. $39 once. No monthly fees, no per-minute charges, no usage caps, no account required. It keeps working even if you never go online again.
Mac only?
For now, yes — macOS 11+ (optimized for Apple Silicon, Intel supported), Premiere Pro 2022–2026. Windows version is in development.
