Frequently asked questions

Is this safe enough for protecting a confidential source?
No footage is uploaded anywhere during transcription, which addresses one specific risk — overall source protection still depends on your full security practices (device security, storage, etc.), not this tool alone.
Can it transcribe phone or field recordings, not just studio interviews?
Yes, though accuracy on lower-quality audio (phone calls, noisy field recordings) will generally be lower than clean studio audio, as with any transcription tool.
Does it identify who's speaking in a multi-person interview?
It transcribes all speech but doesn't label individual speakers (no speaker diarization) in the current version.
