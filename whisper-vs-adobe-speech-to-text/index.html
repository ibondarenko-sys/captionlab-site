The core difference

Adobe's Speech to Text is a proprietary engine tuned for a defined, smaller set of languages. Whisper is an openly published model trained on a very large, multilingual dataset covering 99 languages, and has become a widely used reference point for transcription accuracy across the AI research community.

🌍

Language breadth

Whisper: 99 languages. Adobe Speech to Text: roughly 16, at time of writing.

🎯

Robustness to accents & noise

Whisper's broad training data tends to make it handle accented speech and imperfect audio noticeably well.

🔓

Openness

Whisper is open-source and can run locally; Adobe's engine is proprietary and only available through Adobe's own tools.
