What makes Whisper-based subtitles different

Whisper's training on a huge, varied multilingual dataset tends to make it noticeably better than older speech-recognition engines at handling accents, background noise, and non-English languages — which is the main reason CaptionLab is built on it rather than a narrower, English-first engine.
